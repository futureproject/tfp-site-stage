With Dream Directors now embedded in schools across seven major American cities, The Future Project is unleashing the passion and purpose of over 25,000 students nationwide--and growing. From coast to coast, our Dream Directors have provided tens of thousands of hours of coaching, workshops, and events this school year--and helped empower thousands of incredible young people with big dreams.
