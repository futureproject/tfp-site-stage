Harnessing the Big Apple

The largest school district in the country. Over a million students. A complete playground for innovation and opportunity. We're so excited to have Dream Directors in eight NYC schools this year, and thrilled to represent Brooklyn and Queens (Staten Island, we see you!). The goal is to build a system that taps into the incredible resources this city has to offer its visionary young people.

The NYC Dream Directors

Joyce Gendler

It's her second year as Dream Director at Richard R. Green High School, and Joyce is feeling, subtly but powerfully, that something big could happen this year.

Kleaver Cruz

How cool is it when people are excited about what you're doing before you even started for the year? Dream Director Kleaver had that experience with the students at Leadership Institute High School in the Bronx.

Eleanor Klufio

Dream Director at the NYC Lab School, Eleanor knew that she wanted to create a festive environment for all of the students and schools who call the building home.

Sarah Zapiler

As a first-year Dream Director in a school where we have had a Dream Director before, Sarah knows the power of a good introduction. And the joy of a good smoothie.

Syreeta Gates

A proud native of South Jamaica, Queens, Dream Director Syreeta Gates is bringing the blue to August Martin High School, just steps from her home.

Shantrelle Lewis

Shantrelle lives in Philadelphia, where she will be launching The Future Project's work very soon. While training to unleash the dreams of the City of Brotherly Love, she helped ring in the new (school) year at Hudson High in NYC.

Sarah Katz

Michael Peterson
