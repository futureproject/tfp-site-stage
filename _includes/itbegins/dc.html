Empowering Young Leaders

In a city of such global influence, we're committed to empowering the thousands of young leaders still in high school to discover the power of their own ideas. We're lucky to be working with incredible DC principals, already guiding the re-opening and rebuilding of their schools' campuses and cultures. Together, we'll help the city's young people find their voice on the issues that matter to them most.
