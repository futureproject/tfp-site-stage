Growing with the Elm City

It's our fourth year working with the city of New Haven, and we're honored to be the primary partner of superintendent Garth Harries in increasing the engagement and voice of the city's incredible young people. With more Dream Directors on the ground than ever before, our goals are all about deeper integration: into the curriculum, into the broader school ecosystem, and into the evaluation tools to help us innovate.
