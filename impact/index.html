With Dream Directors now embedded in schools across seven major American cities, The Future Project is unleashing the passion and purpose of over 25,000 students nationwide--and growing. From coast to coast, our Dream Directors have provided tens of thousands of hours of coaching, workshops, and events this school year--and helped empower thousands of incredible young people with big dreams.

We're committed to
measuring what matters.

When we talk about students living lives of passion and purpose decades after their Future Project experience, we mean it; we're obsessed with sustainable inspiration. That's why we worked with our team of academic advisors to understand how to measure what sometimes can seem immeasurable.

After three years of reseach, three categories of metrics rose to the top: the mindset to dream up a better future (will); the tangible abilities necessary to make that future real (skill); and the immersion in a school climate that helps make that ideal future seem possible (thrill). Obviously, we're thrilled that this framework rhymes. But we're even more excited that it reflects what a growing chorus of educators, experts — and students themselves! — are saying matters most in the 21st Century.
